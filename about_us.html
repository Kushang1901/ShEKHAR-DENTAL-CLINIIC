Our Story

Shekhar Dental & Ordhodontic center was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide world-class dental care that combines cutting-edge technology with compassionate, personalized service. What started as a small practice has grown into one of Delhi's most trusted dental clinics.

Dr. Prakash Thakur, our founder and lead dentist, recognized the need for a dental practice that would prioritize patient comfort while delivering exceptional clinical results. Over the years, we have treated thousands of patients, helping them achieve healthy, beautiful smiles that boost their confidence and quality of life.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond just dental procedures. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients, understanding their unique needs, and providing education that empowers them to maintain optimal oral health throughout their lives.

Our Mission & Values

These core principles guide everything we do at Shekhar Dental Clinic.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dental care that improves the oral health and quality of life of our patients through compassionate service, advanced technology, and continuous education. We strive to create positive dental experiences that build lasting relationships and healthy smiles.

Our Vision

To be the leading dental practice in Vadodara, recognized for our excellence in patient care, innovative treatments, and commitment to improving community oral health. We envision a future where everyone has access to quality dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment.
